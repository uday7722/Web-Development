Fitness Tracker

Features
  • Real time activity tracking
  • Heart rate monitoring
  • GPS for accurate distance tracking
  • Calorie counting
  • Water resistent for workouts in any weather
Steps to Use
  1. Wear the fitness tracker on your wrist
  2. Press the power button to turn it on
  3. Connect the tracker to your smartphone using bluetooth feature
  4. Choose your desired activity mode(running,cycling)
  5. Start your workout out the tracker will record your real-time data
  6. Sync the data to mobile app to view detailed insights
